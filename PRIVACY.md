# Be The Penguin Journey — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 2026*

## No account, no personal data

Be The Penguin Journey does not require account creation or sign‑in, and does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

## In‑App Purchases

Purchases (the optional "Energy Shield") are processed entirely by Apple's App Store (StoreKit). We do not receive or store your payment information — it is handled directly by Apple.

## Local data only

The app stores minimal gameplay progress (such as your purchased shield count) locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## Children's privacy

The app collects no data from anyone, including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised by [opening an issue](https://github.com/DevApp11/bethepenguin-support/issues/new) on our support repository.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
